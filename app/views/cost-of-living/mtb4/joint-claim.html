A joint claim gets just one £300 payment

If you had a joint claim between 18 August 2023 to 17 September 2023, and if you were eligible, you should have got a single payment of £300.

 

This is one payment for the joint claim. You will not get £300 each.

 

It should have been sent using the same payment method you used to get Universal Credit between those dates.
