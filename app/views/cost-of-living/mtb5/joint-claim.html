A joint claim gets just one £299 payment

If you had a joint claim between 13 November 2023 and 12 December 2023, and if you were eligible, you should have got a single payment of £299.

 

This is one payment for the joint claim. You will not get 299 each.

 

It should have been sent using the same payment method you used to get Universal Credit between those dates.
