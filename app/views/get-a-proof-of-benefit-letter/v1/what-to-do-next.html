What to do next

If you’re claiming Universal Credit, you can sign in to your online account and save a copy of your Universal Credit statement.

If you're getting any other benefits, or you get Universal Credit but cannot access your online account, you will need to phone the office that pays you to request a proof of benefit letter.

You can find their contact details on a letter they've sent you, or through guidance about the benefit.
