Contact details for the Disability Cost of Living Payment

If you're still unsure whether you should have had the 2023 Disability Cost of Living Payment of £150, contact the office that pays your benefit.

Contact the Disability Service Centre if you get:

  • Disability Living Allowance for adults (DLA)
  • Disability Living Allowance for children (DLA)
  • Personal Independence Payment (PIP)
  • Attendance Allowance (AA)

Contact Social Security Scotland if you get:

  • Adult Disability Payment (ADP)
  • Child Disability Payment (CDP)

Contact Barnsley Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit Centre if you get:

  • Constant Attendance Allowance (CAA) paid with Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit

Barnsley Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit Centre
Telephone: 0800 121 8379
Relay UK (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0800 121 8379
Find out about call charges

Contact Veterans UK if you get:

  • Constant Attendance Allowance (CAA) paid under the War Pensions Scheme
  • Armed Forces Independence Payment
  • War Pensioners' Mobility Supplement (WPMS)

Veterans UK
Telephone: 0808 191 4218
Email: veterans-uk@mod.gov.uk
Monday to Friday: 8am to 4pm
