You're not eligible for a Winter Fuel Payment

You're not eligible for a Winter Fuel payment if you were born on or after 22 September 1959.

You may be able to get other help with heating costs:

  • Warm Home Discount Scheme (opens in new tab)
  • Cold Weather Payment (opens in new tab) (in England and Wales)
  • Winter Heating Payment (opens in new tab) (in Scotland)

You can also check what benefits and other financial support you can get (opens in new tab).
