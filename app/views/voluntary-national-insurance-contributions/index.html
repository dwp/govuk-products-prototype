Request a call back to pay voluntary National Insurance contributions for tax years between 2006 and 2018

Complete this form to request a call back from the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) about paying voluntary National Insurance Contributions to fill gaps in your State Pension entitlement between 2006 and 2018.

The deadline to pay voluntary National Insurance contributions for this period has been extended to the 5 April 2025.

DWP will contact you on the phone number you provide in this form to discuss payment of voluntary National Insurance contributions.
