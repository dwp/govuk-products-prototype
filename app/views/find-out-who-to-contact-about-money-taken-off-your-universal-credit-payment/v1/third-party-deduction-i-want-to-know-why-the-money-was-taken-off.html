Third party deduction - I want to know why the money was taken off

The quickest way to find what has been deducted from your Universal Credit payment is to sign into your online Universal Credit account. Go to your journal and find the message which has this information.

Money can be taken off your Universal Credit payment to pay your debts for:

  • rent arrears
  • service charge arrears
  • court fines
  • child maintenance
  • utilities, like electricity, gas and water
  • Council Tax arrears

You can see on your statement if any money is being taken off for these debts. Look in the section 'What we take off - deductions'. If you need more information about the deduction, contact the organisation you owe money to.

If you need further help, contact Universal Credit by signing into your online Universal Credit account and write a message in your journal.

If you need to call, you may get through quicker if you ring after 10am, Tuesday to Friday.
