Benefit overpayment, budgeting and crisis loan repayment

To find out how a benefit overpayment happened, contact the benefit paying office. For example, if it’s an overpayment of housing benefit, speak to your local council.

Talk to Debt Management about the repayment of benefit overpayment, budgeting and crisis loans.
