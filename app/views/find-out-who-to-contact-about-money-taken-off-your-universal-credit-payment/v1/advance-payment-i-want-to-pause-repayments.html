Advance payment – I want to pause repayments

If you’re struggling to pay your advance, you may be able to pause your repayment for a short time. This is only available in exceptional circumstances.

You need to contact to Universal Credit. The quickest way to do this is to sign into your online Universal Credit account and write a message in your journal.

If you need to call, you may get through quicker if you ring after 10am, Tuesday to Friday.

Universal Credit
