Tax credits and benefit overpayments, budgeting and crisis loan repayments

To find out how a tax credits overpayment happened, contact HMRC (opens in a new tab).

To find out how a benefit overpayment happened, contact the benefit directly. For example, if it's a housing benefit overpayment, speak to your local council.

Make a repayment

The Department for Work and Pensions deals with repayments only.

Contact the Debt Management team about the repayment of:

  • tax credits and benefit overpayments
  • social funds
  • budgeting loans
  • crisis loans
