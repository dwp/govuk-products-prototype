Pension Credit calculator

You can use the Pension Credit calculator

You can use the Pension Credit calculator to check your eligibility and how much you could get.

The calculator will only give you an estimate and should only be used as a guide.

To use the Pension Credit calculator you'll need details of your:

  • earnings, benefits and pensions
  • savings and investments

You'll need the same details for your partner, if you have one.
