About their request

Be as specific as you can. If their request relates to a particular benefit or payment, please say what this is.

Request for CCTV or Body Worn Camera footage

If their request is for CCTV or Body Worn Camera footage from a DWP office or Jobcentre, we must be able to identify them in the recording. To help us locate the footage, please include:

  • the name of the building or location where the recording took place
  • the date and approximate time they want the footage from
  • a brief description of what happened
  • a description of the citizen, including what they were wearing at the time
