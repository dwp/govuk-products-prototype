Send a personal information request for a citizen

People have the right to get a copy of the information that is held about them. This is called the 'right of access'.

Use this form to send a request on a citizen's behalf if they cannot, or will not, complete the request themselves on GOV.UK.

Before you start

Do not complete this form for:

  • proof of someone's entitlement to benefits
  • a copy of their benefit rates and dates
  • copies of letters or forms

Use this form for more complicated personal information requests. This could be where personal information:

  • is held across different benefit systems over long periods of time
  • needs specialist handling by data protection staff

Other third parties will need to prove they have the authority to act on a citizen's behalf. They can do this by writing to DWP using the personal information request form for third parties instead.
