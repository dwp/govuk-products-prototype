Tax credits

If you think you should have had the £301, £300 or £299 payment for tax credits from HMRC by now, but you cannot see it in your bank, building society or credit union account, you can contact HMRC to check.

Telephone: 0345 300 3900
Relay UK if you cannot hear or speak on the phone: dial 18001 then 0345 300 3900
Monday to Friday: 8am to 6pm

If you get tax credits from HMRC and a low income benefit from DWP, you will get a Cost of Living Payment from DWP only.
